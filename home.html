I'm Kim Portnoy, a passionate and hard-working design leader who's spent the last 20 years creating delightful digital products.

Work

I lead Product Design at Sycle, a software platform for managing audiology practices.

I manage a team of two rockstar user experience designers who's optimism and enthusiasm drive me to be a better leader.  I am always asking for their input, trust their judgement and appreciate their dedication to improving the team and themselves immensely. 

Previously, I lead the User Experience at Bitly. Prior to that I spent over 12 years as a freelance designer, with clients ranging from well known brands to early stage startups. I spend a few years working for Dell honing my front-end development skills and learning about Information Architecture. In the late 90's I was busy designing recruiting brochures for many of NYC's top law firms .

I strive to create an environment that fosters personal and professional growth by preaching what I practice, listening more, and continuously self-assessing.
